The applet below shows several ways to visualize the electric field produced by a group of charges. The slider alters the magnitude and sign of some of the charges in the simulation window. If the visualization mode is set to "Mouse Makes Field Lines," changing the slider value clears the field lines. If the visualization mode is set to "Mouse Drops Field Vectors" or "Mouse Makes Field Lines," double-clicking will clear all the vectors.
